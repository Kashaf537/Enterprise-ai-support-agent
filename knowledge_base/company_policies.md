TechNova Cloud — Company Policies

## Data Retention
Active workspace data is retained indefinitely while the account is active. Upon account deletion, data is retained for 7 days (grace period for recovery) then permanently purged within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

## Data Privacy
TechNova Cloud is GDPR and SOC 2 Type II compliant. Customer data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We do not sell customer data to third parties. Data processing agreements (DPAs) are available for Business and Enterprise customers upon request.

## Service Level Agreement (SLA)
- Business plan: 99.9% uptime guarantee. Credits issued if breached: 10% monthly credit per 0.1% below guarantee, up to 50% credit.
- Enterprise plan: 99.99% uptime guarantee with custom credit terms defined in the contract.
- Free and Pro plans: best-effort uptime, no formal SLA.

## Acceptable Use Policy
Customers may not use TechNova Cloud for: cryptocurrency mining, hosting malware, sending unsolicited bulk email, or any activity violating applicable law. Violations result in immediate suspension without refund.

## Support Hours
- Free tier: community forum only, no guaranteed response time
- Pro: email support, Monday-Friday 9am-6pm UTC, 24 hour response target
- Business: priority email and chat, 24/5, 4 hour response target
- Enterprise: 24/7 phone and chat support, 1 hour response target

## Escalation Policy
A support interaction is escalated to a human agent automatically when: the AI's confidence score falls below 30 percent, the customer explicitly requests a human, the issue involves account security (e.g. suspected unauthorized access), or the issue involves a refund dispute that has already been denied once.

## Change Management
Major platform changes are announced at least 14 days in advance via email and the in-app changelog. Security patches may be deployed without advance notice when necessary.
