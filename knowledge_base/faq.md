# TechNova Cloud — Frequently Asked Questions

## What is TechNova Cloud?
TechNova Cloud is a SaaS platform for deploying, monitoring, and scaling cloud-native applications. It provides container orchestration, automated CI/CD pipelines, and real-time observability.

## How do I create an account?
Go to signup.technova.cloud and register with your work email. You will receive a verification email — click the link within 24 hours to activate your account. After activation, you can log in and create your first workspace.

## How do I invite team members?
Navigate to Settings > Team > Invite Members. Enter their email address and select a role (Admin, Developer, or Viewer). They will receive an invitation email with a link valid for 7 days.

## What regions does TechNova Cloud support?
We currently support us-east-1, us-west-2, eu-west-1, eu-central-1, and ap-southeast-1. You can select your preferred region during workspace creation, and it cannot be changed afterward without migrating to a new workspace.

## How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your registered email. You will receive a reset link valid for 30 minutes. If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

## Can I use TechNova Cloud with my existing CI/CD tools?
Yes. TechNova Cloud integrates with GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, Jenkins, and CircleCI via webhooks and our deployment API.

## What happens if I exceed my plan's resource limits?
You will receive an email warning at 80% usage. At 100% usage, new deployments are throttled but existing services continue running. Upgrade your plan anytime from Billing > Subscription to lift the limit immediately.

## Is there a free trial?
Yes, all new accounts get a 14-day trial of the Pro plan with no credit card required. After the trial, you can choose to downgrade to the Free tier or enter billing details to continue on Pro.

## How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings > Account > Danger Zone > Delete Account. This action is irreversible and permanently deletes all workspaces, deployments, and data after a 7-day grace period during which you can cancel the deletion.

## Do you offer Single Sign-On (SSO)?
SSO via SAML 2.0 and OAuth (Google, Microsoft, Okta) is available on the Business and Enterprise plans.
