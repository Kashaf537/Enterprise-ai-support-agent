# TechNova Cloud — Pricing Guide

## Plans Overview

### Free Tier — $0/month
- 1 workspace
- 2 deployments
- 1 GB storage
- Community support only
- 60 API requests/minute

### Pro — $49/month per workspace
- Unlimited deployments
- 50 GB storage
- Email support with 24-hour response time
- 600 API requests/minute
- Up to 10 team members

### Business — $199/month per workspace
- Everything in Pro
- 500 GB storage
- Priority support with 4-hour response time
- 3000 API requests/minute
- Unlimited team members
- SSO (SAML/OAuth)
- 99.9% uptime SLA

### Enterprise — Custom pricing
- Everything in Business
- Dedicated infrastructure options
- Custom rate limits
- 24/7 phone support with 1-hour response time
- 99.99% uptime SLA
- Dedicated customer success manager

## Billing Cycle
All plans bill monthly on the date of signup. Annual billing is available with a 20% discount, billed upfront.

## Payment Methods
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and ACH bank transfer for Business and Enterprise plans. Invoicing (NET-30) is available for Enterprise customers only.

## Upgrading or Downgrading
You can change plans anytime from Billing > Subscription. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated for the remainder of the billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

## Overage Charges
If you exceed your plan's storage or API limits, overage is billed at:
- Storage: $0.10/GB over limit
- API requests: $5 per additional 1000 requests/minute of sustained overage

## Failed Payments
If a payment fails, we retry 3 times over 7 days. After the third failed attempt, the workspace is downgraded to Free tier (deployments remain but no new ones can be created) until payment is updated.
