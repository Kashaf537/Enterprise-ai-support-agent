TechNova Cloud — Refund Policy

## Eligibility
Refunds are available within 14 days of a charge for the following reasons:
- Accidental duplicate charge
- Service was unusable due to a confirmed platform outage lasting more than 4 consecutive hours
- Customer upgraded by mistake and contacts support within 48 hours of the upgrade

## Non-Refundable Situations
- Charges older than 14 days
- Partial month usage when downgrading or cancelling (we do not pro-rate cancellations)
- Overage charges that were correctly billed for actual usage
- Enterprise contracts, which are governed by the signed Master Service Agreement instead of this policy

## Refund Calculation
For eligible refunds, the amount is calculated as:
refund_amount = (days_remaining_in_billing_cycle / total_days_in_cycle) * amount_charged

For example, a $199 charge on a 30-day cycle with 20 days remaining at the time of refund request yields:
(20 / 30) * 199 = $132.67

Outage-based refunds are calculated as a credit equal to the proportion of downtime: (outage_hours / 720) * monthly_charge, capped at one full month's charge.

## How to Request a Refund
Contact support via the in-app chat or support@technova.cloud with your invoice number and reason. Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days back to the original payment method.

## Dispute Process
If a refund request is denied, customers may escalate to a billing specialist by replying "escalate" to the denial email. Disputes are reviewed within 3 business days.
